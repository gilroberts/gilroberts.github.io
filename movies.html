My Personal Favorite Thrillers/Horror Movies :)
The Shining: The Shining is known as possibly the best horror movie ever made and is my favorite movie of all time. It however, uses both elements of horror and thriller making it a mix of both. Iconic.
Hereditary: My favorite horror movie classified as "supernatural horror". Watching this alone in the dark for the first time was terrifying and Ari Aster is a genius.
