Notable Works

"Crack is Wack" - 1986

Inspired by the crack epidemic in New York City in the 1980's, Haring created this street art on a handball court at 128th and 2nd avenue. Even though he originally created it without the permission of the city, it is now protected by the city.

"Untitled(Dance)" - 1987

Keith Haring is known for creating numerous works under the name of "Untitled". If you google Keith Haring Untitled 7 different ones come up with different years but there are many many more. Among them is Dance.

"Ignorance=Fear" - 1989

All of Haring's work has symbolism behind it. There is even a site that breaks down the hidden language within his art. This one of course, is less hidden as it speaks its truth loud and clear.
